Scam Alert

नया ऑनलाइन स्कैम: OTP मांगने वालों से सावधान

July 15, 2026 5 min read 2.5K views

Cyber criminals are constantly finding new ways to steal money from innocent people. A new scam has emerged where fraudsters ask for OTP to empty your bank account.

Alert: No bank employee ever asks for your OTP over the phone.

How Does This Scam Work?

Scammers call you pretending to be a bank representative. They say your bank account is going to be blocked and ask you to share an OTP to prevent it.

As soon as you share the OTP, they transfer money from your account.

How to Stay Safe?

  • Never share your OTP with anyone.
  • If someone asks for OTP, hang up immediately.
  • Banks never ask for OTP over the phone.
  • If in doubt, contact your bank directly.
Tip: Save your bank's official number and ignore calls from unknown numbers.

What to Do If You Become a Victim?

If you have accidentally shared your OTP, call your bank immediately to freeze your account. Also, file a complaint with the Cyber Cell.

Cyber Cell Helpline: 1930

Remember: Your safety is in your hands. Stay aware, stay safe.
