Our Mission

Astra QR Generator is a free, easy-to-use tool for creating beautiful QR codes. Whether you need to share a URL, contact information, or any other data, our tool makes it simple and fast.

Features

  • ✓ Generate QR codes instantly
  • ✓ Customize colors and size
  • ✓ Download in PNG and SVG formats
  • ✓ Choose error correction level
  • ✓ Copy QR code to clipboard
  • ✓ 100% free to use

Why Astra QR Generator?

We believe in making powerful tools accessible to everyone. Our QR generator is designed with simplicity and functionality in mind, so you can create the perfect QR code in seconds. All processing happens locally in your browser - no data is ever sent to our servers.

Privacy First

Your privacy matters to us. All QR codes are generated locally on your device. We do not store or track any data you enter. Everything stays on your computer.
